Common questions

Things people
always ask first.

If your question isn't here, just WhatsApp it. We answer fast.

What if I don't like the video?
You get one free revision — tell us what's off and we redo the affected parts. If you still don't like it after the revision, you don't pay. We'd rather refund than ship a video you'd be embarrassed to post.
What do I need to send you?
Three things, all in WhatsApp: (1) what your business does in one or two lines, (2) what you want viewers to do after watching — visit, call, order, follow, (3) any photos, logo, or footage you have. If you have none, that's fine — we'll generate what we need.
Can you do it in Indonesian / another language?
Yes. Bahasa Indonesia, English, and most major languages are fine — just tell us which one (and which dialect/accent for voiceover) in your first message.
How many revisions are included?
One. We've found that one well-aimed round of changes is enough for the vast majority of orders. If you need more after that, each extra revision is $5.
Is the 24-hour turnaround real?
Yes — we count from when we have everything we need (your brief and any assets). If we're slammed on a given day we'll tell you upfront, but the default is 24 hours and we hit it on the large majority of orders.
What formats do I get?
Vertical 9:16 (Reels, TikTok, Shorts, Stories) by default. Need square 1:1 or horizontal 16:9 for a website or YouTube? Mention it in WhatsApp — same price.
